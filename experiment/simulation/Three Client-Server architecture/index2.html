Three-Tier Client Server Architecture

Instructions

  • Send Request: Click the "Send Request" button to enter your name, email, and message. You'll see an animation showing data flowing from the client to the server. After completing all fields, press the "Enter" key to begin the request animation.
  • Send Response: After sending the request, click "Send Response" to simulate a server reply back to the client. Once all response fields are filled, press the "Enter" key to send the response animation.
  • Fetch Data: Enter your email address to retrieve the data you previously submitted.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear the form and restart the animation process.
  • Print: Click "Print" to generate a print-friendly version of the page layout and the data currently displayed.
